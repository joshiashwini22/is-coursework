Research


To successfully vision and develop the website for the bookstore, research was conducted. References were taken from different webpages and websites on the internet. Here are five distinct fetures that were inspired from the research and implemented in the project.

Navigtion Bar | W3 Schools


W3 Schools had a navigation bar where the active page could be seen and when a certain page's nav was hovered on, the colour of that part changed.




Shop now banner | Bohemian Traders


The banner has a background image. The store's name and a shop now button are included in the image. The shop now button redirects to the product page.




Product Grid | Ekta books


The product grid in the website of Ekta books had 4 products in one row. The product area included book cover, book title, author, and price.




Blog Intro| The Hustle


The blog intro presents a brief interface of the blog content. This includes the title and author of the book and other relevant materials.




Contact Section | Ekta books


The contact us section includes a form and a location map. Send is a submit button that checks whether full name, email and message area is filled or not.
